Frequently Asked Questions

Does health insurance cover laser treatment?

Yes! Laser surgery for proctology conditions is approved by USFDA and fully covered by almost all major government and private health insurance providers. We provide complete cashless processing support.

Is laser surgery painful?

Laser operations use intense, concentrated beams of light instead of traditional surgical knives. This avoids cutting muscle fibers, resulting in 90% less post-op discomfort than standard open surgeries.

How many days does complete recovery take?

Most patients are perfectly stable for discharge within 24 hours of treatment. You can walk around comfortably right away and safely resume normal light working desk tasks in 2 to 3 days.

Is there any food restriction after treatment?

For the first few weeks, our specialists recommend a high-fiber diet alongside plenty of water intake to ensure soft bowel movements. Avoid heavily spicy or deep-fried food structures during healing.
